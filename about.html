The Plants

At the heart of the development of iPlant lie the plants. Our researchers and engineers collaborated in order to bring you a system that knows your plants and their needs, and will meet them. Our iPlant ecosytem is optimized for all of your indoor plant needs.

The iPlant Sensors

The iPlant uses some of Heptagon's finest sensors to provide you with the best experience. The sensors in the iPlant detect temperature and humidity, ambient light and a variety of compounds in the air including carbon dioxide and VOCs.
